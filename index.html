Miller Projects

FSCW Department Software, This software system will be a web based system for the FSCW Department and will have two modules, i.e.FPS Module and Miller Module. This system will be designed to keep detailed record of the inspector's inspection in their respective allotted demography. One of the key benefits of the system is that it makes it possible to compare the values of Physical Balance and Book Balance of the items being inspected. This system will increase the efficiency of the inspector and will assist higher authorities in the Department in getting detailed view of the data in different formats as desired, which would otherwise have to be performed manually. With the availability of detailed data without much difficulty, it will be helpful in decision making for higher authorities of the department. Inspectors will upload Physical Inspection data of their respective allotted Fair Price Shops(FPS) and Government Mills for Rice & Wheat processing plants. System will generate different reports with the data submitted by the inspector. Each inspector will be provided with separate user ID and password to enter data. A super-user can view data of entire state of Odisha.
· There are two basic users – Super user, User.
· All users have their own profiles in Inspector Module.
